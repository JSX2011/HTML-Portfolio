Movies and Series Ranking by Juan Duque

This movie ranking display my favorite movies and series from 1 to 3 of all time.

1. Mr. Nobody

Basicaly a guy living a normal shitty life but with good music and a nice narration.

2. The Witcher - Complete Series

Basically, the best looking guy in Hollywood (for the mid 30's women and older) with long white hair and a long silver sword slaying monsters and pus... Witches, altogether. Any man's dream, basically.

The Last Of Us - Complete Series(TLOU for the friends)

Post Apocalyptic world where a bad-ass guy lose his daughter and starts building his career as a survivor mercenary. The history, actors and scenaries are accurate to the game with some minor but well done changes that I enjoyed watching a lot.
